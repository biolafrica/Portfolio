Eatup Food Services Limited

JavaScript / Node.js / Express.js / Ejs / Mongo DB

Overview

EatUp Food Services Blog is an engaging blog platform designed to enhance the online presence of the food delivery brand. Built with a robust stack of JavaScript, Node.js, and Express.js, the blog provides a seamless and user-friendly experience for both visitors and administrators. The visually appealing design, crafted in Figma, ensures that users enjoy a delightful interface while exploring various culinary topics, recipes, and food delivery tips. With a focus on community engagement, the blog serves as a hub for food enthusiasts, sharing insights and fostering connections around the joy of food.

Key features

• Responsive Design: The blog is designed to be fully responsive, ensuring optimal viewing on various devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

• Dynamic Content Management: Utilizing Node.js and Express.js, the blog allows for dynamic content updates, providing fresh and relevant articles to readers.

• EJS Templating: The use of EJS (Embedded JavaScript) enables efficient rendering of HTML pages, making it easy to display blog posts and manage content dynamically.

• Admin Dashboard: The dedicated admin dashboard allows authorized users to log in, create, edit, and delete blog posts, ensuring efficient content management.

• User-Friendly Interface: The intuitive design, developed in Figma, enhances user experience, allowing visitors to navigate easily through various categories and posts.

• Engaging Content: The blog features a diverse range of topics, including recipes, food trends, and delivery tips, aimed at engaging the audience and promoting the EatUp brand.
