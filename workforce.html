Overview

Workforce is a comprehensive employee management platform designed to streamline staff sign-in, attendance tracking, and salary monitoring for companies. As the lead on both design and development, I was responsible for the entire process, from conceptualizing the platform in FigJam and Figma to implementing the front-end with React and the back-end using Node.js and Express.js.
