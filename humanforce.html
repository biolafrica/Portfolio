Workforce Project

React / Node.js / Express.js / Mongo DB

Overview

Humanforce is a comprehensive employee management platform designed to streamline staff sign-in, attendance tracking, and salary monitoring for Eatup Food Services Limited. As the lead on both design and development, I was responsible for the entire process, from conceptualizing the platform in FigJam and Figma to implementing the front-end with React and the back-end using Node.js and Express.js. Humanforce provides a user-friendly and efficient way for Eatup Limited to manage their staff’s work hours, attendance, and payroll, all in one integrated system.

Key features

• Responsive Design: The HumanForce web app is fully responsive, ensuring seamless access across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

• Employee Management System: Provides a centralized platform for managing employee records, payroll, attendance, and work schedules efficiently.

• Dynamic Payroll Processing: Automates payroll calculations for both fixed and contract staff, ensuring accurate salary and wage disbursement.

• Scheduled Payroll Emails: Uses automated scheduling to generate and send payroll slips via email at designated times, enhancing efficiency and transparency.

• PDF Payslip Generation: Generates professional payslips in PDF format using Puppeteer, allowing employees to easily view and download their payment details.

• Admin Dashboard: A secure dashboard for HR and management to oversee employee data, approve leaves, and manage financial records.

• Secure Authentication: Implements robust authentication and authorization mechanisms to protect sensitive employee and company data.

• Attendance & Shift Tracking: Monitors employee work hours and shift schedules, ensuring efficient workforce management.

• Scalable Backend: Powered by Node.js and Express.js, with MongoDB as the database, ensuring scalability and fast performance.
